Ana Davis

My name is Ana, a few call me by my middle name, Cecilia. I was born in Sonora, Mexico. My hometown, San Carlos is about 6 hours away from Tucson, and it is great place to visit. I grew up in Mexico City, where I met amazing people and visited wonderful places. I currently live in the lovely Tucson, AZ. I have managed to fall in love with this town, their food, traditions and even their weather.

"Familia" and God comes first to me. My husband and three kids make me very happy, let's say I am very blessed to have them. I am slightly obsessed with sharks and cactus. I enjoy the beach, food, art and good music.
